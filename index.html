About Us

Founded in 2018, Cozy Cup Cafe was born from a simple dream: to create a warm, welcoming space where people could escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, wanted to bring the charm of European coffee houses to our neighborhood.

We source our beans from sustainable, fair-trade farms around the world and roast them locally to ensure the freshest, most flavorful cup every time. Our pastries are baked fresh daily by our talented team of bakers who pour love into every creation.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a quiet moment with a good book, we're here to make your day a little brighter, one cup at a time.
